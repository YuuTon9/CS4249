FOOD DRIVES
It's a daily struggle to feed the stray & shelter animals. Help us by buying one of food bundles or buy directly through one of our supported pet food  suppliers.

​Beneficiaries are:
Doggie Rescue Shelter, Exclusively Mongrels, Gentle Paws, Just Kindness,  K9 Missions, Blk 2 Furever Canines, Oasis Second Chance Animal Shelter (OSCAS), Uncle Khoe's, CAS Adoption Centre, CAS street animals & other independent dog & cat feeders

​
CHEAPEST FOOD
Food is a basic necessity for shelters.  To save on costs, often the cheapest food is brought for the animals.

Unfortunately cheaper foods tend to have limited nutritional value, are full of fillers and contain food that dogs and cats shouldn't eat.
MEDICAL ISSUES FROM DIET
A common problem for long term shelter dogs is kidney failure, diabetes and other diet related illnesses due to a cheap, bad diet.

Once a dog gets this sick, it's expensive to maintain their well being.
NUTRITIONAL DIET
Good food is essential to helping our shelter and street animals keep healthy.  Healthy animals can cope better within the shelter environment and cope with shelter-related stresses.
