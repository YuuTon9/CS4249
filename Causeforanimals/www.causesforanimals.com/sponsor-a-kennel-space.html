CAS KENNELS
Causes for Animals Singapore have secured kennel space at Seletar and Tengah to help save our CAS street dogs who are losing their homes and face the threat of culling.
Our kennels can house puppies, dogs, cats and include two isolation kennels for new dogs and dogs staying under our Trap, Neuter and Release Programmes
We also have two run spaces and access to a large training ground for our dogs.
We will use these kennels to help rescue, rehabilitate and rehome our Singapore Specials
